This is a very early version of this tool! In particular, it's not optimized at all for mobile, desktop use is strongly recommended. Some more features waiting to get added:
  • A view of the Archon Card, with and without enhancements.
  • A grid view of all the cards in the deck.
  • The ability to sample random starting hands.
  • The ability to see some basic counts (number of aember pips, etc).
  • Accurate rendering of enhancement pips, as well as accurate houses of mavericks, directly on the card images.
  • Performance improvement.
  • A visual overhaul.
EYEGOR
Eyegor is a simple tool which lets you explore a deck digitally in a similar manner to how you do it physically--without the distraction of SAS numbers or Master Vault jank. Just the cards, please!
